Meanings of the Collected Data

BPM (Heartbeats per Minute)

This measure indicates the number of your heart beats per minute. It can vary depending on your emotional state, your level of physical activity, and your overall health. For instance, a high BPM may reflect excitement, while a low BPM could indicate calmness.

Color of Your Soul

This visual representation of your inner aura is based on the analysis of your biometric data. Each color may reflect a specific emotion or state of mind. It's important to note that several dominant colors can coexist, each bringing its unique meaning:
Blue: Serenity, inner calm, peace.
Red: Excitement, passion, energy.
Green: Healing, personal growth, balance.
Orange: Creativity, enthusiasm, warmth.
Purple: Spirituality, intuition, transformation.
Yellow: Joy, optimism, mental clarity.
Pink: Love, compassion, harmony.
White: Purity, transcendence, enlightenment.

Current Emotion

This measure reflects the dominant emotion you are feeling at the moment, deduced from your biometric data. This can help you better understand your current emotional state.

Age of the Soul

This symbolic value represents the maturity of your soul, calculated in years. A higher soul age may indicate profound wisdom and increased spiritual understanding.

Karmic Debt

This measure suggests the amount of "karmic debt" your soul may carry, a symbolic notion of unfinished lessons or unresolved experiences from past lives. A high karmic debt may mean a need to resolve conflicts or spiritual challenges.
